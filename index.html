Our Story

A Legacy of
Sweetness

Founded in 1998 by Chef Priya Nair on the cobbled lanes of Bandra, Fig & Flour began as a tiny kitchen with a big dream — to bring the art of authentic French patisserie to Mumbai's discerning palate.

Today, with three locations across the city, we remain guided by our original promise: exceptional quality, local sourcing, and the belief that every dessert tells a story.
