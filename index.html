Our Story

A Legacy of Sweetness

Founded in 1998 by Chef Priya Nair on the cobbled lanes of Bandra, Fig & Flour began as a tiny kitchen with a big dream — to bring the art of authentic French patisserie to Mumbai's discerning palate.

Today, with three locations across the city and a devoted following of over 20,000 loyal customers, we remain committed to the principles that guided us from the very first day: exceptional quality, the finest ingredients, and the belief that every dessert tells a story.

Behind the Scenes

From Kitchen to Your Table

🌙
4:00 AM — Prep
Our bakers arrive before sunrise to begin laminating croissant dough and tempering chocolates.
🥚
5:30 AM — Mixing
Seasonal fruits are washed. Batters are folded with care. Each recipe is weighed to the gram.
🔥
6:30 AM — Baking
Our deck ovens are hand-set for each product. Croissants bake in rotating stone. Cakes in convection steam.
🎨
8:00 AM — Finishing
Each cake is iced, glazed and decorated by hand. Every macaron is filled one-by-one with our signature ganaches.
✨
9:00 AM — Display
Items are styled and placed in our glass display. Doors open, and a new day at Fig & Flour begins.
