Our Story

A Legacy of
Sweetness

Founded in 1998 by Chef Priya Nair on the lanes of Bandra, Fig & Flour began as a tiny kitchen with a grand dream — to bring authentic French patisserie to Mumbai.

Today, three locations across the city, 20,000+ loyal guests, and every single item still made by hand before sunrise.
